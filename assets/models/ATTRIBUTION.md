## Simple Skin

The packaged `simple_skin.glb` is a minimal glTF skinning conformance asset,
repackaged from the Khronos glTF Sample Assets repository's multi-file
`SimpleSkin.gltf` into a single-buffer `.glb` container (Glint's loader only
reads the binary GLB format) — geometry and math are unmodified.

- Copyright 2017 Marco Hutter, CC0 1.0 Universal
- Source: https://github.com/KhronosGroup/glTF-Sample-Assets/tree/main/Models/SimpleSkin
